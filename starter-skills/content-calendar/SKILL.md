# content-calendar (paid)

Generates a thirty-day content calendar for a client across the social
platforms the operator has connected. Picks topics from the operator's
brief, balances post types across the week, and queues drafts for human
review.

## Tier
Paid — included in every paid bundle.

## Triggers
The agent invokes this skill whenever the operator asks for a content
calendar, a posting schedule, or a thirty-day plan.

## Inputs
- A client brief — voice, audience, monthly themes.
- The list of connected platforms.
- A posting cadence — posts per week per platform.

## Outputs
- A calendar in the operator's planning directory.
- One draft per scheduled slot, queued for human review.

## Compatibility
Hermes default; OpenClaw fork; BYOA Codex; BYOA Claude Code.
